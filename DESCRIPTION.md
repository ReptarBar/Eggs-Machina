# Thunderbird Eggs Machina

**Build whimsical Rube Goldberg contraptions and watch your egg journey through delightful chaos!**

A fun, open-world physics sandbox where you create elaborate chain-reaction machines using snap-together components. Every outcome is a celebration - even failures are delightful!

## Key Features

- **Whimsical & Fun**: Every egg carries a random emoji and celebrates any outcome
- **Rich Parts Library**: Walls, platforms, springs, fans, motors, pendulums, and more
- **Thunderbird Integration**: Leaderboards, achievements, and community features
- **Emoji-Based UI**: Beautiful, intuitive interface with emoji icons
- **Open-World Building**: Create contraptions of any size and complexity
- **Journey Tracking**: Measure how far your egg travels
- **Achievement System**: Unlock badges for creative contraptions

## How to Play

1. **Build**: Drag parts from the toolbox to create your contraption
2. **Customize**: Use keyboard controls to rotate, tilt, and activate components
3. **Play**: Click "Play" and watch your egg journey through your creation
4. **Celebrate**: Every outcome is a win - even failures show delightful animations!

## Building Tools

### Interactive Parts
- **Ramps**: Guide your egg's path
- **Planks**: Create bridges and platforms
- **Wheels**: Add rolling elements
- **Hinges**: Create moving connections
- **Dominos**: Chain reaction triggers

### Static Objects
- **Walls**: Barriers and boundaries
- **Platforms**: Landing surfaces
- **Blocks**: Solid obstacles
- **Springs**: Bouncy launchers
- **Levers**: Mechanical triggers

### Propulsion Systems
- **Fans**: Blow your egg around
- **Pendulums**: Swinging forces
- **Motors**: Continuous propulsion
- **Tiltable Platforms**: Adjustable slopes

## Controls

- **Mouse**: Drag and drop parts, select objects
- **R Key**: Rotate ramps
- **Arrow Keys**: Tilt platforms
- **Spacebar**: Toggle fans and motors
- **Delete/Backspace**: Remove selected objects

## Thunderbird Community Features

- **Shared Leaderboards**: Compete with other Thunderbird users
- **Newsletter Integration**: Stay updated on Thunderbird news
- **State of Thunder**: Quick access to Thunderbird updates
- **Community Features**: Share your creations with the Thunderbird family

## Philosophy

**"It's about the journey, not the destination!"**

Every egg carries a surprise emoji, and every outcome - whether success or failure - is celebrated with confetti, sparkles, and joy. Build for the fun of building, experiment with wild contraptions, and enjoy the delightful chaos of physics in action.

Perfect for creative problem-solving, stress relief, learning basic physics concepts, family fun, and artistic expression through engineering.
